Adjective Quiz for Grade 2

Story 1: The Big Dog

There is a big dog named "Ball". Ball has brown fur and long hair. It loves to run in the garden every day. One day, it met a small dog named "Mimi". Mimi has white fur and short hair. The two dogs played together happily.

1. What is Ball like?
small big thin
2. What color is Ball's fur?
white brown black
3. What is the small dog's name?
Ball Mimi Fufu
4. What is Mimi's fur like?
long short curly
5. Where do the dogs play together?
in the house in the garden at school

Story 2: The Cat and the Fish

There is a cat named "Maw". Maw has black fur and yellow eyes. It loves to sit near the fish tank. The fish tank has big and small fish. The big fish is orange, and the small fish is blue. Maw always watches the fish.

6. What is Maw like?
white black brown
7. What color are Maw's eyes?
blue yellow green
8. What color is the big fish?
blue orange green
9. What size is the small fish?
big small thin
10. What does Maw do near the fish tank?
play watch eat

Story 3: The Happy Elephant

There is a happy elephant named "Ellie". Ellie is big and gray. It loves to splash water in the pond. One day, Ellie saw a thin rabbit. The rabbit was hopping beside the pond. Ellie invited the rabbit to play in the water.

11. What is Ellie like?
thin happy small
12. What color is Ellie?
gray brown white
13. What does Ellie love to do?
jump splash water run
14. What is the rabbit like?
big thin fat
15. Where did Ellie invite the rabbit?
to the house to the water to the forest
